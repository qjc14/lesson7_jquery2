Asperagus

Ingredients

  • 1 lb asparagus
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/4 tbsp salt
  • 1/4 tbsp pepper
  • 1/2 tsp flavoring of your choice

Instructions

Stovetop medium heat.

Put olive oil in pan.

Chop off bottom inch of asparagus (hard roots).

Mix in salt pepper and flavoring.

sitr asparagus occasionally and place lit on pan for solid simmer.

cook on stovetop for about 7-9 minutes.
